Your Data Stays With You

Capio is built on a simple principle: your planning data belongs to you, stays on your devices, and is never accessed by us.

Your planner content never leaves your device unless you have iCloud Sync enabled — in which case it goes to your own private iCloud container, which we cannot access. We have no servers storing your planning data. We do not track you. We do not sell your data. We do not run ads.

What Capio Stores Locally

  • All planner content — tasks, notes, time blocks, sprint goals, handwriting
  • App settings and preferences
  • Onboarding completion state
  • Trial start date and status
  • Subscription status

What Capio Does Not Collect

  • No name, email, or contact information
  • No location data
  • No usage analytics or behavioral tracking
  • No crash reports sent to Capio servers
  • No device identifiers or fingerprinting
  • No advertising identifiers (IDFA)
  • No third-party analytics SDKs
  • No in-app advertising of any kind
  • No data shared with or sold to third parties

Apple ID Sign-In

You sign in with Apple to start your free trial. Your Apple sign-in token is sent once to our server, which keeps only a one-way hash of your Apple ID and the trial start date — never your name or email — so each Apple ID gets one free trial. We also use your Apple ID to link your planners, verify subscription status, and enable iCloud sync. We do not use your email for marketing.

iCloud Sync

If iCloud Sync is enabled, your data lives in your private iCloud container — encrypted, invisible to Capio and Apple. This enables sync between your iPad and iPhone. Disabling iCloud Sync keeps all data local to your device only.

Handwriting & Text Recognition

All handwriting recognition runs entirely on your iPad using Apple's on-device neural engine. Your handwriting is never transmitted anywhere.

Notifications & Reminders

All reminders are scheduled locally using Apple's notification system. No notification content is ever sent to any external server.

Payments

Capio never sees your payment details. Stripe and Apple handle all payment processing under their own privacy policies. Capio receives only a subscription confirmation — never your card number, billing address, or financial information.

Risk: Data Loss

Deleting the app removes all local data permanently. With iCloud Sync on, your data restores automatically when you reinstall and sign back in with the same Apple ID. Without iCloud Sync, back up your iPad regularly — Capio cannot recover data on your behalf.

Your Rights

Right to access: Your data is on your device. You can see it anytime.

Right to deletion: Delete the app to remove all local data permanently. For your iCloud copy: iPad Settings → Your Name → iCloud → Manage Storage → Capio → Delete Data.

Right to portability: Your data exists in SwiftData on your device. Data export is planned for a future version.

GDPR (EU residents): For any GDPR-related inquiries, contact vee@capioplan.com. We will respond within 30 days.

CCPA (California residents): Capio does not sell personal information. For CCPA inquiries, contact vee@capioplan.com.

Changes to This Policy

If Capio's data practices change in a future version — for example, if we add optional cloud features — this policy will be updated and a prominent in-app notification will be shown before the change takes effect. You will never be opted into new data practices without notice.
